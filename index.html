Biography

Chris Mastel is a Calgary, Alberta based full-stack developer. After spending time as an engineer in the oil and gas industry, it was time to learn some new skills. Taking courses in Data Analytics and Artificial Intelligence confirmed his interest in the programming world, but there seemed to be something missing. By taking the Web Development Bootcamp through Lighthouse Labs, he was able to lay the foundations of a new career in software development.

Whenever he isn't in front of a computer, you can generally find him on some sort of adventure in the Rocky Mountains. Backpacking, canoeing, and skiing are the perfect balance to the world of software development!
